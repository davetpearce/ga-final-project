A little more about me...

I'm a Kiwi, but live in country Victoria, Australia with my partner Dave and cat Lucy in a rammed earth home. I don't have kids by choice. I’m an introvert and lover of animals and 80’s power ballads (nothing gets my toes tapping than a bit of Africa by Toto!).

Before copywriting, I graduated from Victoria University in Wellington with a law degree. I began my career practising family law and then worked in various legal policy roles within the public sector.

I then made the massive career shift into copywriting and entrepreneurship, which has led to so much more personal and professional growth than I could ever have imagined.
